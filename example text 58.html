The Story in the book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, Lying betweeen the copies of Nature, New Scientist. and all the other Scientist journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On The road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in the room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiminf that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considrered her pioneering work, unraveling the secrets of the llama Dna, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
